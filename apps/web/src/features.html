Manager multiple twitter accounts

Step #1: Multwiple allows you to easily manage multiple twitter accounts. If you are a first time user, goto the home page and click on the "Sign-In with Twitter" button. This will redirect you to Twitter.


Step #2: Here you need to enter your credentials to sign-in using one of your twitter accounts. Make sure you click on the Allow button.

Step #3: This action will again redirect you back to multwiple.com. This time you will be able to see your tweet updates, direct messages and mentions.

Step #4: Now, to add another twitter account, simply click on the + (plus) sign at the end of the tabs.

You will now see the "Sign-In with Twitter" button again. Clicking this will again take you back to twitter where you add sign-in using your other twitter account.


Step #5: Please note that you might have to sign-out from your previous twitter account to sign-in with the new account. Once you have entered the required details, click on Allow button to return back to multwiple.com.

This time you will find a new twitter account (new tab) added next to the previous one. There is no restrictions on how many twitter accounts one can add.

URL Shortener

Squeezed for space in tweets? Have a long URL you must include in your tweet? Not to worry. Use the URL shortener. Click on the "URL Shortener" link, paste your long URL and click on the Add button to add the shortened URL to your tweet. Works like a charm!


  
Search for Tweets

Step #1: Want to know who else is talking about your product or your brand? Want to know who is using a particular client? You can now simply search for it.

Step #2: Searching a keyword in user tweets all over the world couldn't have been any easier. Simply type in your keyword in the search boxes shown in top-left of the menu and click "Go". A new tweet box will appear showing all the tweets containing your keyword.

Note: To search a keyword in tweets tweeted using a particular twitter client, use the following format: <keyword>:<client>. For example, Burger:multwiple will results in tweets containing the word 'Burger' that were tweeted using multwiple.

Tweet follows your Link

Yes! Its actually true! Now you can never loose track of a tweet or the link contained in it! Whenever you click on a link in a tweet box in multwiple, the link is opened in a new tab/browser window with the original tweet shown at the top.


Re-Tweet: This way if you wish, you can re-tweet that tweet quite easily. Let go off all your worries of remembering which link came out of which tweet!! Save your time and be in command!

View Statistics

Simple to keep track the number tweets or your favorite tweets or your followers or people you follow. Its all there!

Current Trends!

Know what the current trending topics are. Keep abreast with the rest of the tweeties! Trends allow youto quick check on new trending topics and follow people tweeting about them.
